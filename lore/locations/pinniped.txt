
Pinniped

Pinniped is the capital of the Seal Kingdom.

It is situated around a small river, near Mount Black.

Pinniped was first founded in the year 192 BP by the ancient Seals.

Pinniped contains Castle Slavoneska, were the monarchs of the Seal Kingdom exist.

Pinniped is the second most populated city in the Seal Kingdom after Monachinae.

Pinniped is considered to be the most ancient city in the known world.
